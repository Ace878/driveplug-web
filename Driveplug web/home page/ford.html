Ford Mustang Mach-E

The Ford Mustang Mach-E is an all-electric SUV that combines the iconic Mustang performance with modern electric technology.

About the Car: With a range of up to 300 miles and 0-60 mph in as little as 3.5 seconds, it’s designed for thrill-seekers.

performance:

  • Range: Up to 300 miles
  • 0-60 mph: As quick as 3.5 seconds (GT model)
  • Horsepower: Up to 480 hp
  • Torque: Up to 634 lb-ft
  • Battery: 88 kWh (standard), 98.8 kWh (extended range)

Price:Starting at $43,995

Ford F-150 Lightning

The Ford F-150 Lightning is an all-electric version of America’s best-selling truck, offering impressive power and capability.

About the Car: With a range of up to 300 miles and a towing capacity of over 10,000 pounds, it’s built for work and play.

performance:

  • Range: Up to 300 miles
  • 0-60 mph: As quick as 4.0 seconds (Lightning model)
  • Horsepower: Up to 563 hp
  • Torque: Up to 775 lb-ft
  • Towing Capacity: Up to 10,000 pounds

Price:Starting at $39,974

Ford Explorer

The Ford Explorer is a versatile SUV that offers a range of powertrains, including hybrid and plug-in hybrid options.

About the Car: With seating for up to seven and advanced safety features, it’s perfect for families.

performance:

  • Engine Options: 2.3L EcoBoost I-4, 3.0L EcoBoost V6
  • Horsepower: Up to 400 hp (ST model)
  • Towing Capacity: Up to 5,600 pounds
  • Seating Capacity: Up to 7 passengers
  • Fuel Economy: Up to 28 mpg highway (hybrid)

Price:Starting at $36,760
