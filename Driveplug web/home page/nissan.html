Nissan Leaf

The Nissan Leaf is one of the world’s most popular and affordable electric cars, known for its practicality and efficiency.

About the Car: With up to 212 miles of range (Leaf Plus), zero emissions, and advanced driver-assist features, it's a smart choice for city and suburban driving.

performance:

  • Range: up to 212 miles
  • Power: 147 hp (standard), 214 hp (Leaf Plus)
  • Battery: 40 kWh (standard), 62 kWh (Leaf Plus)
  • Charging: 0-80% in 40 minutes with DC fast charging

Price: $29,990 - $36,990

Nissan Ariya

The Nissan Ariya is a sleek, all-electric SUV that combines performance, comfort, and futuristic design.

About the Car: With a range up to 304 miles, dual-motor AWD, and a spacious tech-filled cabin, the Ariya is Nissan's flagship EV SUV for the modern driver.

performance:

  • Range: up to 304 miles
  • Power: 214 hp (single motor), 389 hp (dual motor)
  • Battery: 63 kWh (standard), 87 kWh (long range)
  • Charging: 0-80% in 30 minutes with DC fast charging

Price: $40,000 - $60,000

Nissan Sakura

The Nissan Sakura is a compact kei electric car designed for urban environments with style and efficiency in mind.

About the Car: With a lightweight design, nimble handling, and a range perfect for daily commutes, the Sakura is Japan’s city-friendly electric ride.

performance:

  • Range: up to 180 km (112 miles)
  • Power: 47 hp
  • Battery: 20 kWh
  • Charging: 0-80% in 40 minutes with DC fast charging

Price: $14,000 - $18,000
