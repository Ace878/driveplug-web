Audi Q4 e-tron

The Audi Q4 e-tron is a compact electric SUV that combines cutting-edge tech with Audi’s signature luxury feel.

About the Car: With up to 265 miles of range, an intuitive digital cockpit, and a spacious interior, it’s designed for both comfort and performance.

performance:

  • Power: Up to 295 HP
  • 0-60 mph: 6.2 seconds
  • Range: Up to 265 miles

Price: $48,000

Audi e-tron GT

The Audi e-tron GT is a powerful electric grand tourer that delivers stunning performance and jaw-dropping design.

About the Car: With up to 522 horsepower (or 637 HP in RS trim), 0-60 in just over 3 seconds, and quattro AWD, this EV is a true performance machine.

performance:

  • Power: Up to 637 HP (RS model)
  • 0-60 mph: 3.1 seconds (RS model)
  • Range: Up to 238 miles
  • Top Speed: 155 mph

Price: $99,900 - $142,700

Audi Q8 e-tron

The Audi Q8 e-tron is a premium electric SUV that offers refined performance, space, and all-around sophistication.

About the Car: With a range up to 285 miles, dual electric motors, and a comfortable high-tech interior, it’s the flagship of Audi’s electric SUV lineup.

performance:

  • Power: Up to 402 HP
  • 0-60 mph: 5.6 seconds
  • Range: Up to 285 miles
  • Top Speed: 124 mph

Price: $70,000 - $90,000
