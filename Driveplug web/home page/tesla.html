Tesla Model 3

The Tesla Model 3 is a compact electric sedan that blends range, performance, and cutting-edge tech at an affordable price.

About the Car: With up to 358 miles of range and a 0-60 mph time as quick as 3.1 seconds (Performance model), the Model 3 offers Autopilot and full self-driving capability. It’s a top choice for both city and highway driving.

Price: $35,990 - $56,990

Tesla Model S

The Tesla Model S is a luxury electric sedan known for its incredible speed, long range, and refined interior.

About the Car: Offering over 400 miles of range and the option for the insane “Plaid” model (0-60 in under 2 seconds), it’s packed with tech features like a 17-inch touchscreen, yoke steering, and advanced safety systems.

Price: $94,990 - $129,990

Tesla Model X

The Tesla Model X is a futuristic SUV with iconic falcon-wing doors, spacious seating, and serious performance.

About the Car: It boasts a range of up to 348 miles and a 0-60 mph time of 2.5 seconds (Plaid). It includes room for up to 7 passengers and is one of the safest SUVs ever tested.

Price: $99,990 - $119,990
